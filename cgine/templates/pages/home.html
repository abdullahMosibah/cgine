What is ?

Platform that enables online-learners receive educational material in a concise and self-paced form.

knowledge blocks

each lesson consist of knowledge blocks. but what is knowledge block ?

think of it as a slide that contains some related information. it's a simple way to organize knowledge to better understand the lesson

Text,Video and Audio material

each knowledge block contains 3 types of content Text, Video, and audio

to enhance understanding and learning. all those three types are synced together to create an interactive learning experience.

Quick google/google scholar search shortcuts

by double-clicking on any highlighted text

you can either search that text on google or google scholar and play the related video/audio part that explains it

Quizzes

after any knowledge block you can add a quiz in order to examine yourself

this way you learn actively rather than passively, which will ultimately enhance your learning experience
